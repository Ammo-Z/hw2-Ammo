# HW2: LLM Meeting Summarizer for Hardware Supply Chain

## Business Workflow
**Workflow Chosen:** Summarizing complex hardware engineering and supply chain sync meetings into structured action items.

**Target User:** Hardware Project Managers (PMs) and Global Supply Managers (GSMs).

**Input:** Raw, messy transcripts of voice meetings involving mechanical engineers, suppliers (e.g., CNC machining, PCB assembly), and quality control teams.

**Output:** A structured Markdown list of Action Items, including the Directly Responsible Individual (DRI), Due Date, and specific deliverables (e.g., updated BOM, DFM feedback).

**Value Proposition:** Hardware supply chain meetings are dense, filled with acronyms (MOQ, DFM, PCBA), and often involve rapid decision changes (e.g., swapping a component due to lead times). Missing an action item can delay manufacturing builds (EVT/DVT) by weeks. Automating the initial extraction saves PMs hours of manual review and reduces human error.
